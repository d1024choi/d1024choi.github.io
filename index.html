About

I work on machine learning and computer vision for autonomous driving, with a focus on motion forecasting, planning, and bird's-eye-view perception from surrounding images.

Research Interests

  • Motion Forecasting and Planning
  • 3D Perception from Monocular Images
  • Autonomous Driving
  • Machine Learning and Computer Vision

Current Roles

  • Principal Researcher at ETRI (Feb 2017 - Present)
  • Associate Professor at UST (Mar 2023 - Present)
